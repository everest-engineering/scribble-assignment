# Quickstart: Gameplay Interaction

## Manual Validation

1. Start a two-player game.
2. As drawer, draw on the canvas.
3. Confirm drawing appears on the drawer screen and syncs after polling.
4. Clear the canvas as drawer.
5. Confirm a non-drawer cannot clear.
6. Submit an empty guess and confirm validation.
7. Submit an incorrect guess and confirm it appears with 0 score change.
8. Submit the correct word with different casing and confirm 100 points.
9. Confirm guess history appears for all players after polling.
